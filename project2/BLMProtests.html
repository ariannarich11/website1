Black Lives Matter Protests

What is Black lives matter?

Black Lives Matter (BLM), is an organization dedicated to the improvement of the black communty’s safety and well-being. As described in their website:

“Black Lives Matter Global Network Foundation, Inc. is a global organization in the US, UK, and Canada, whose mission is to eradicate white supremacy and build local power to intervene in violence inflicted on Black communities by the state and vigilantes. By combating and countering acts of violence, creating space for Black imagination and innovation, and centering Black joy, we are winning immediate improvements in our lives.”
(Black Lives Matter)

Why Was 2020 an Important
Year for BLM?

COVID-19 shutdowns had forced the U.S into quarantine, shutting down schools, jobs, public places of recreation and etc. As Americans were stuck at home, more eyes were glued to the screen in hopes of updates on the virus. However, with the increase of viewership, news reports on police brutality were put on blast to a greater audience. Graphic videos of people suffering at the hands of officers went viral, not just in the U.S, but also across the globe as well. And as George Floyd’s death exposed the horrors of police brutality in such a vivid way, the video documenting it had incited outrage on an international scale. Because more Americans had become face-to-face with these videos more people sought to protest, calling for police reform and justice for those who had died. But Amereicans weren’t the only ones who seeked justice, protests had emerged in many countries around the world including: Denmark, Italy, Britain, France and many more.
